Child Safety Policy

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Effective Date: March 29, 2026  |  Contact: safeedpromo@gmail.com

Moon Chat has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. We are fully committed to the safety and protection of minors and actively work to prevent any misuse of our platform.

1. Age Requirement

Moon Chat is strictly intended for users aged 18 years and older. Users under the age of 18 are not permitted to use this app. By using Moon Chat, users confirm they are at least 18 years old.

If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately removed and their data deleted.

2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following content and behaviors are strictly prohibited on Moon Chat:

  • Any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors (CSAM)
  • Grooming, soliciting, or attempting to exploit minors in any way
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing any sexually explicit content involving minors
  • Using the platform to arrange or facilitate harm to a child
  • Any communication that sexualises individuals under the age of 18

Violations will result in immediate permanent account termination and will be reported to relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where required by law.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

We provide clear and accessible ways for users to report CSAE content or suspicious behaviour:

  • In-app reporting: Users can report any chat session using the Report button available in every chat screen
  • Email: Reports can be sent directly to safeedpromo@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly. We take every report seriously and act immediately upon confirmation of a violation.

4. Enforcement

Upon receiving a report or detecting a violation, Moon Chat will:

  • Immediately terminate the offending user's access
  • Preserve relevant data for law enforcement if required
  • Report confirmed CSAM to NCMEC's CyberTipline and cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies
  • Take additional steps as required by applicable law

5. Our Commitment

Moon Chat is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We continuously review our platform and policies to ensure compliance with child safety standards, including those outlined by Google Play, Apple App Store, and applicable laws worldwide.

We support the efforts of organisations working to combat CSAE, including the Internet Watch Foundation (IWF) and NCMEC.

6. Contact

To report CSAE content or concerns, or for any child safety related enquiries:

Email: safeedpromo@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all child safety reports within 24 hours.
