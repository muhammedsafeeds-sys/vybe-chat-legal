## Host These Publicly Before Play Submission

Files ready to host:
- `docs/privacy-policy.html`
- `docs/terms.html`

Simple hosting options:
- GitHub Pages
- Netlify
- Cloudflare Pages
- Your own website/domain

After hosting:
1. Copy the public HTTPS privacy policy URL
2. Copy the public HTTPS terms URL
3. Replace the placeholders in `lib/core/constants/app_config.dart`

Do not submit to Play Console with placeholder URLs.
