Vybe Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Vybe Chat is an anonymous social chat service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when it may be shared, and the choices available to users. Anonymous use does not mean zero data processing. Technical and content-related data may still be processed to operate, secure, and moderate the service.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous profile data such as veil name, veil style, streaks, spark points, and acceptance of policy versions.
  • Authentication and technical identifiers needed to maintain an anonymous account session, including anonymous authentication identifiers, app installation data, push notification tokens, and device-level diagnostics.
  • User-generated content including chats, confessions, reports, ratings, and other content you choose to submit.
  • Moderation and safety data such as report reasons, moderation flags, automated abuse checks, and actions taken to protect users or the service.
  • Advertising, analytics, crash, and infrastructure data from integrated providers including Google, Firebase, and Supabase.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain your anonymous account and app profile.
  • To match users, deliver chats, ratings, confessions, and related features.
  • To detect, prevent, investigate, and respond to abuse, spam, fraud, harassment, unlawful conduct, or safety risks.
  • To restrict, suspend, or terminate accounts or sessions that violate our rules or create risk for other users.
  • To improve app quality, security, reliability, and feature performance.
  • To comply with law, enforce our Terms, and respond to valid legal requests.

3. Anonymous Use and Safety Limits

Vybe Chat is designed for anonymous interaction, but anonymity is not absolute. We may retain account-linked technical identifiers, safety signals, reports, and service records to help enforce the rules, investigate abuse, protect users, and comply with law. You should not share sensitive personal, financial, medical, or identifying information in chats or confessions.

4. Moderation and Reports

We may use automated filtering and human review where permitted to detect restricted content, spam, scams, off-platform solicitation, threats, or other prohibited conduct. Reports made by users may be stored and used to investigate behavior, apply moderation actions, and support account restrictions or suspensions.

5. Legal Bases and Service Operation

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we process data to provide the service you request, protect the safety and security of users and systems, comply with legal obligations, and pursue legitimate interests such as fraud prevention, abuse mitigation, and service improvement.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with infrastructure, hosting, messaging, analytics, advertising, moderation, or security providers as needed to operate the app. We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary to enforce our policies, investigate misuse, protect users, or comply with legal obligations.

7. Third-Party Services

Vybe Chat may rely on third-party services including Google AdMob, Firebase, Google Play services, and Supabase. Those providers may process data under their own policies and terms.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase Privacy Policy

8. Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, investigate abuse, enforce our Terms, support moderation decisions, comply with law, resolve disputes, and protect users and the platform. Some data may be retained longer when necessary for safety or legal reasons.

9. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. No system can guarantee absolute security, and you use the service at your own risk.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own depending on the providers and infrastructure used to operate Vybe Chat.

11. Children and Age Limits

Vybe Chat is not intended for children and should only be used by individuals who meet the minimum age required by applicable law and platform policy. If you believe a child has used the service unlawfully, contact us so we can review the issue.

12. Your Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may control notification permissions through your device settings.
  • You may request support using the contact details you publish with the service.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we may update the effective date and publish the revised version. Continued use of the service after changes are posted may indicate acceptance of the updated version where permitted by law.
