Last updated: March 26, 2026

Vybe Chat is an anonymous social chat app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Anonymous profile details such as your veil name and selected veil style.
  • Technical identifiers used to keep your anonymous profile working, such as app-generated device or installation identifiers.

  • Notification-related identifiers such as push messaging tokens, if notifications are enabled.
  • Advertising and analytics-related data provided by Google AdMob, Firebase, or other platform services.
  • Basic device, app, and diagnostic information needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and maintain your anonymous app profile.
  • Match users for chat sessions.
  • Deliver messages, confessions, ratings, streaks, and similar app features.
  • Send notifications if you have enabled them.
  • Show and manage advertisements.
  • Detect abuse, policy violations, fraud, or misuse.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

3. Anonymous Use

Vybe Chat is designed around anonymous interaction. You are not asked to provide your real name in normal use of the app. However, content you choose to submit, including chat messages or confessions, may still be stored and processed to provide the service and moderate misuse.
